With this sample you can create a shared AR experience at any of our Niantic Wayspots -- without using Unity's Netcode solution.

<b>Instructions</b>:
Add your API key in the Lightship > Settings menu.
(Editor) Assign the playback dataset for the desired Wayspot in Project Settings->Niantic Lightship SDK

* Enter your user name in the text field
* Set a radius and tap <b>Search</b>
* Choose a wayspot and tap the <i>Localize</i> button.
* Physically go to the location youâ€™ve chosen and point the camera at the location in the hint image.
* After a few seconds you should connect to the network and see any other connected peers.
